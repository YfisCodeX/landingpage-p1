Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MakeApp (the “App”, “we”, “us”), operated by [Your legal name], handles information when you use the App. MakeApp is a selfie-based makeup assistant: you provide a photo and your preferences, and the App returns AI-generated makeup recommendations and a preview image.

We built the App to be privacy-respecting by default. We do not run analytics or tracking SDKs, we do not show ads, and we do not sell your data.

1. Information we process

  • Your photo. The selfie you take, or the photo you choose from your library, is sent over an encrypted connection through our processing service to our AI providers (Anthropic and OpenAI) solely to generate your makeup analysis and preview image. Your photo is used only to produce your result. It is not stored on our servers after processing, is not used to train AI models, and is never used for advertising, tracking, profiling, facial recognition, or identification.
  • Your results. The analysis text and generated preview images are stored locally on your device only. We do not keep a copy on a server.
  • A random device identifier. The App generates a random identifier (a UUID) stored securely on your device to manage your subscription and usage credits. It is not your name, email, Apple ID, phone number, or an advertising identifier, and it is not linked to your real-world identity.
  • Purchase and subscription status. Purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat. We receive your subscription/credit status — not your payment card or full billing details.
  • Limited diagnostic logs. Our processing service may keep transient technical logs (for example, error and timing information) to keep the service working. These are not used to identify you.

2. How we use information

  • To generate your makeup analysis and preview image.
  • To manage your subscription, one-time credit packs, and remaining usage.
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To keep the App secure and working correctly.

3. What we never do

We do not sell or rent your data; we do not use your photo for advertising, tracking, profiling, facial recognition, biometric templates, or identification; and we do not create user profiles for marketing.

4. Service providers we share data with

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to operate the App. They process data on our behalf and only as needed to provide their part of the service:

  • Anthropic (makeup analysis) — privacy policy
  • OpenAI (preview image generation) — privacy policy
  • Cloudflare (our processing/proxy service hosting) — privacy policy
  • RevenueCat (subscription management) — privacy policy
  • Apple (App Store and payments) — privacy policy

5. Storage and retention

Your photos, results, and saved looks are stored on your device. We keep your random device identifier and credit balance on our service for as long as you use the App so your purchases work across sessions. Photos are processed transiently by our AI providers and are not retained by us afterward.

6. Your choices and rights

  • Delete your data. In the App, go to Profile → Privacy & Data → “Delete all saved looks & history” to remove your locally stored results and images.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • Depending on where you live (for example, under GDPR or CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data and to opt out of sale (we do not sell data). To make a request, contact us at the email below.

7. Children

MakeApp is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

8. International transfers

Our providers are located primarily in the United States, so your information may be processed there. We rely on appropriate safeguards where required.

9. Security

Data in transit is protected with encryption (HTTPS/TLS), and the device identifier is held in your device’s secure storage. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date above, and significant changes may be highlighted in the App.
